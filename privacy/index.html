Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we process

Tender documents you submit for analysis. They are processed in memory and discarded immediately after analysis. No document content is stored, logged, or retained.

What we log

Anonymous usage counts and average response times. No personally identifiable information, no IP addresses, no document content.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. When using hosted mode, document text is sent to the AI model provider for processing; in direct/BYO-key mode, nothing leaves your infrastructure.
